# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 21 April 2026*

GymLedger ("we", "our", "the app") is a workout tracking application operated by Rohit Saluja, based in Jaipur, India. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

## 1. Data We Collect

When you use GymLedger, we collect only the data required to run the app:

- **Account information**: email address and (if you sign in with Google) your Google account ID and display name, used to identify your account and sync your data.
- **Workout data**: exercises, sets, reps, weights, and workout templates you create.
- **Body metrics**: body weight entries you log.
- **Subscription status**: whether you hold an active GymLedger Pro subscription, received from Apple via RevenueCat.
- **Device and crash data**: if the app crashes, we may receive anonymized crash reports through our error monitoring service.

We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, microphone, health data from Apple Health, or any device identifiers for advertising.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to:

- Let you sign in and access your workout history across devices.
- Sync your entries in real time.
- Verify your subscription status.
- Fix bugs reported via crash logs.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except the infrastructure providers listed below, and only to the extent needed to operate the app.

## 3. Service Providers

GymLedger relies on the following third-party services:

- **Supabase** ([supabase.com](https://supabase.com)) — hosts our database and handles authentication. Your workout data and account info are stored here.
- **Apple / Google Sign-In** — used for authentication only; we receive your email and name, nothing else.
- **RevenueCat** ([revenuecat.com](https://revenuecat.com)) — processes subscription entitlements. RevenueCat receives a user identifier (your account ID) and your subscription purchase events.
- **Apple (App Store Connect)** — processes all payments. We never see your card details.

Each provider has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

## 4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time by emailing us (see Contact below). On deletion, your workout history, account, and associated data are permanently removed within 30 days.

## 5. Children

GymLedger is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have, please contact us and we will delete it.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address below.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be surfaced in-app and the "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the change.
